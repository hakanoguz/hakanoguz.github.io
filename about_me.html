About me

I work as a professor in the Department of Landscape Architecture at Kahramanmaras Sutcu Imam University. My area of expertise includes GIS, urban remote sensing, urban heat island effect, urban sprawl, thermal remote sensing, urban growth, landuse/landcover change, and integrating information technology to resource management.
